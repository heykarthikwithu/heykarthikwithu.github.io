Chain snatching is one of the fastest-growing street crimes across India. It happens suddenly, often within seconds, and leaves the victim shocked, shaken, and sometimes even injured. The criminals involved in chain snatching usually work in pairs or small groups and target vulnerable individuals in public spaces. Unlike bigger crimes like robbery, burglary, or house break-ins, chain snatching is quick, easy, and requires very little planning — making it a preferred method for many petty criminals.
