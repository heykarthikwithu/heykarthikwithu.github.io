Ashada masam (Nija Ashadamasam) is the 4th month in Telugu calendar of Andhra Pradesh & Telangana. In 2019, Ashada masam starts on July 3 and ends on August 1 as per the Telugu Panchangam. Similar dates are observed as Ashada masa in Kannada, Marathi, and Gujarat Amavasyant Chandramana Panchanga.
