The Origin of Coconut Tree

Monday, July 22, 2019 - 08:19

Let us enjoy reading this Hindu Mythological Story of The Origin of Coconut Tree. King Trishanku was a saintly man whose one great desire was to ascend bodily into heaven.He had once done a good turn to sage Vishwamitra and the sage decided to help him fulfill his desire.
