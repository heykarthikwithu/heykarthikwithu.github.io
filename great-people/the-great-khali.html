The Great Khali – Renowned Indian Professional Wrestler, Actor and Former WWE World Heavyweight Champion

Listen Pause Resume Stop

The Great Khali, born Dalip Singh Rana on August 27, 1972, is one of the most iconic figures in the world of professional wrestling. Known for his towering height, immense physical strength and charismatic personality, he became the first Indian-born WWE World Heavyweight Champion, inspiring millions across India and the globe.

Early Life and Background

Dalip Singh Rana was born in Dhiraina, a village in the Sirmaur district of Himachal Pradesh, India. Coming from a humble family, he displayed exceptional physical strength at a young age. His early life included:

  • Growing up in a financially modest household
  • Working multiple jobs including as a security guard
  • Developing interest in sports, strength training and wrestling

His dedication to physical training led him to pursue professional wrestling—a decision that changed his life forever.

Initial Training & Wrestling Debut

The Great Khali's wrestling journey began in the early 2000s.
He trained under the guidance of Indian wrestling legend The Great Gama, and his extraordinary size and physique quickly gained the attention of international wrestling promoters.

This led to opportunities in the United States, where he joined professional wrestling circuits before entering WWE.

Debut in WWE (2006)

The Great Khali made his WWE debut in 2006, entering the ring as a dominant and intimidating force. His first major storyline was with The Undertaker, instantly making him one of the most feared superstars in WWE.

Why he stood out in WWE:

  • Height: 7 ft 1 in (216 cm)
  • Weight: Around 157 kg
  • Extraordinary strength
  • Larger-than-life persona
  • Unique in-ring dominance

He quickly became a central figure in major storylines and earned global recognition.

WWE World Heavyweight Champion (2007)

In 2007, The Great Khali won the WWE World Heavyweight Championship, becoming the first Indian-born superstar in history to win a world championship title in WWE.

This milestone not only elevated his career but also inspired millions of Indian fans and aspiring athletes.

Notable Feuds and Matches

Throughout his WWE career, he competed with top stars including:

  • John Cena
  • The Undertaker
  • Triple H
  • Batista
  • Big Show
  • Kane

His matches were known for his raw power and dominant presence in the ring.

He also participated in Royal Rumbles, pay-per-view matches and international WWE events.

Acting and Media Career

Beyond wrestling, The Great Khali built a successful career in films and television.

Bollywood Movies
He appeared in several Hindi films such as:

  • Kambakkht Ishq (2009)
  • Rama: The Saviour (2010)
  • Kuselan (Tamil)

Hollywood & International Films
He also featured in international projects like:

  • The Longest Yard (2005)
  • Get Smart (2008)

His acting roles further increased his recognition worldwide.

Television & Reality Shows
Khali has appeared in multiple Indian TV shows, reality programs and guest appearances, making him a mainstream entertainment figure.

Philanthropy and Public Engagement

The Great Khali is actively involved in supporting social causes including:

  • Health initiatives
  • Education programs
  • Sports encouragement for youth

He also founded the Continental Wrestling Entertainment (CWE) academy in India to train young wrestlers and support aspiring athletes.

Personal Life

Family
The Great Khali is married to Harminder Kaur.
They have a daughter, and his family has been a strong pillar of support throughout his career.

Age, Height & Lifestyle

  • Age: 52 (as of 2024)
  • Height: 7 ft 1 in (216 cm)
  • Weight: 157 kg+
  • Lifestyle: Disciplined fitness routine, strength training and wellness-focused living

His strict lifestyle is one of the key reasons behind his exceptional longevity in sports and entertainment.

Legacy and Impact

The Great Khali is widely regarded as:

  • A trailblazer for Indian wrestlers
  • A global icon who opened doors for future Indian talent in WWE
  • A symbol of strength, determination and perseverance
  • An inspiration to athletes worldwide

His crossover success in wrestling, films and television has made him one of the most influential Indian sports personalities of all time.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is The Great Khali’s real height?
The Great Khali is 7 ft 1 in (216 cm) tall, making him one of the tallest WWE wrestlers in history.

2. When did The Great Khali join WWE?
He made his WWE debut in 2006, instantly becoming a major attraction.

3. What are The Great Khali’s major achievements?
His biggest achievement is winning the WWE World Heavyweight Championship in 2007.

4. Has The Great Khali acted in movies?
Yes, he has appeared in Bollywood films like Kambakkht Ishq and international films like The Longest Yard and Get Smart.

5. Is The Great Khali still wrestling?
He occasionally makes special appearances and currently focuses on training young wrestlers through his CWE academy.

Conclusion

The Great Khali’s journey from a small village in Himachal Pradesh to becoming a WWE World Heavyweight Champion is one of the most inspiring stories in Indian sports history. His achievements in wrestling, acting and philanthropy reflect his dedication, strength and global impact.
