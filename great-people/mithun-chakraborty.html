Mithun Chakraborty

On9th Nov 2023, 2025-11-19T15:00:27+05:30 Listen Pause Resume Stop

Mithun Chakraborty, born Gourang Chakraborty on June 16, 1950, is a celebrated Indian actor, dancer and former Member of Parliament. He is known for his versatile acting skills, energetic dance performances and his contributions to Indian cinema. Mithun Chakraborty's journey in the film industry spans several decades and he has a significant fan following both in India and internationally.

Here are some key points about his life and career:

  1. Early Life and Entry into Films: Mithun Chakraborty was born in Kolkata, West Bengal. He initially worked as a junior artist in the film industry before gaining recognition for his performances.
  2. Breakthrough and Stardom: Mithun's breakthrough came with the film "Mrigayaa" (1976), for which he won the National Film Award for Best Actor. This marked the beginning of his successful acting career.
  3. Disco Dancer: Mithun Chakraborty gained immense popularity as a dancing sensation with the film "Disco Dancer" (1982). His dance moves and the film's music became iconic, leading to his nickname, "Disco King."
  4. Variety of Roles: Mithun Chakraborty is known for his versatility and has portrayed a wide range of characters in films. He has worked in action, drama, comedy and socially relevant movies.
  5. National and International Fame: He became a popular actor not only in India but also in several other countries, particularly in the former Soviet Union.
  6. Awards and Recognition: Mithun Chakraborty has won several awards, including three National Film Awards. He has also received the Filmfare Lifetime Achievement Award.
  7. Parallel Cinema: Apart from commercial films, Mithun Chakraborty has also been associated with the parallel cinema movement and has worked in critically acclaimed films like "Mrigayaa" and "Tahader Katha."
  8. Dance and Stunts: His energetic dance performances and action sequences in films contributed to his mass appeal.
  9. Television and Reality Shows: Mithun Chakraborty has also been associated with television, including as a judge on reality shows like "Dance India Dance."
  10. Political Involvement: Mithun Chakraborty was a Member of Parliament (MP) in the Rajya Sabha (Upper House of the Indian Parliament) from 2014 to 2016.
  11. Philanthropy: He has been involved in various philanthropic activities, including supporting educational initiatives.
  12. Legacy: Mithun Chakraborty's impact on Indian cinema and entertainment is immense. He remains a beloved and respected figure in the film industry.

Mithun Chakraborty's dynamic performances, unique dance style and contributions to Indian cinema have made him an enduring figure in the world of entertainment. His diverse roles, both on-screen and off-screen, have earned him a special place in the hearts of fans across generations.
