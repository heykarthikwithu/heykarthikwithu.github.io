Master C V V

Master C V V (Chintamani Vishveshwara Vaidya) was a renowned Indian scientist and Ayurvedic physician. He was born on February 15, 1898, in Maharashtra, India and passed away on October 29, 1990.

Kishore Kumar

Kishore Kumar, born as Abhas Kumar Ganguly (August 4, 1929 – October 13, 1987), was an iconic Indian playback singer, actor, lyricist, composer, producer, director and screenwriter. He is one of the most celebrated and versatile artists in the history of Indian cinema.

Rabindranath Tagore

Rabindranath Tagore (1861-1941) was a multifaceted Indian polymath, poet, philosopher, musician, artist and Nobel laureate. He was born on May 7, 1861, in Calcutta, British India (now Kolkata, India). Tagore's contributions to literature, music and social reform have had a profound and enduring impact, both in India and globally.
