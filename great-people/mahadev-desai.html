Mahadev Desai

Listen Pause Resume Stop

Mahadev Desai (1892–1942) was an Indian independence activist, writer and close associate of Mahatma Gandhi.

Here's an overview of his life and contributions:

  • Early Life: Mahadev Haribhai Desai was born on January 1, 1892, in Saras, a village near Porbandar, Gujarat, India. He came from a family with a history of social and political activism.
  • Education: Desai studied at Gujarat College in Ahmedabad, where he came in contact with prominent political figures and was influenced by nationalist ideas.
  • Association with Mahatma Gandhi: Desai became acquainted with Mahatma Gandhi in 1917 and soon became one of his closest aides and confidants. He was deeply influenced by Gandhi's philosophy of nonviolence and Satyagraha.
  • Role in the Indian Independence Movement: Mahadev Desai actively participated in various aspects of the Indian independence movement, including organizing protests, leading campaigns and promoting constructive programs like khadi (handspun cloth) and village industries.
  • Secretary to Mahatma Gandhi: Desai served as Mahatma Gandhi's personal secretary for more than two decades, from 1917 until Gandhi's death in 1948. He played a crucial role in managing Gandhi's correspondence, organizing his schedule and assisting him in various activities.
  • Writing: Mahadev Desai was an accomplished writer and journalist. He wrote extensively on Gandhian philosophy, Indian history and social issues. He served as the editor of several newspapers and periodicals, including "Navajivan," the weekly publication founded by Gandhi.
  • Imprisonment: Desai was arrested several times for his involvement in the independence movement and spent a significant portion of his life in prison. He endured hardships and made sacrifices for the cause of Indian freedom.
  • Legacy: Mahadev Desai's contributions to the Indian independence movement and his close association with Mahatma Gandhi earned him respect and admiration from contemporaries and future generations alike. He is remembered as a dedicated and selfless patriot who played a significant role in shaping the course of Indian history.

Mahadev Desai's life exemplifies the spirit of sacrifice, dedication and commitment to the cause of Indian independence. His writings continue to inspire people to this day, providing insights into the ideals and principles of the Gandhian philosophy.
