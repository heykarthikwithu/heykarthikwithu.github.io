Bangalore to Dandeli Solo Trip Plan

On5th May 2024, 2025-11-20T12:39:17+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Dandeli promises an exciting adventure amidst nature, wildlife and thrilling activities.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Day 1: Bangalore to Dandeli

  • Morning: Departure from Bangalore
    • Start your journey early in the morning to make the most of your day.
    • The distance between Bangalore and Dandeli is approximately 460 kilometers and the drive takes around 8-9 hours.
  • Late Morning/Afternoon: Arrival in Dandeli
    • Check into your accommodation in Dandeli.
    • Freshen up and relax for a bit after the journey.
  • Afternoon: River Rafting
    • Head out for an adventurous river rafting experience on the Kali River.
    • Enjoy navigating through the rapids amidst the picturesque surroundings.

  • Evening: Wildlife Safari
    • Embark on a wildlife safari in the Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary.
    • Keep an eye out for sightings of wildlife such as deer, bison and various bird species.

Day 2: Explore Dandeli

  • Morning: Trek to Syntheri Rocks
    • Start your day with a trek to Syntheri Rocks, a spectacular rock formation amidst lush greenery.
    • Explore the caves and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Late Morning/Afternoon: Visit Dandeli Backwaters
    • Spend some time relaxing by the tranquil backwaters of Supa Dam.
    • You can indulge in activities like kayaking, boating, or simply enjoy the peaceful ambiance.
  • Evening: Visit Kavala Caves
    • Explore the ancient Kavala Caves, known for their stalactite and stalagmite formations.
    • Marvel at the natural beauty and learn about the historical significance of the caves.

Google Ad 2

Day 3: Departure

  • Morning: Bird Watching
    • Start your day early with a bird watching session in the Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary.
    • Spot a variety of bird species in their natural habitat.
  • Late Morning/Afternoon: Visit Shiroli Peak
    • Trek to Shiroli Peak for panoramic views of the surrounding forests and valleys.
    • Enjoy the serene ambiance and capture some breathtaking photographs.
  • Afternoon: Return Journey to Bangalore
    • Check out from your accommodation.
    • Start your journey back to Bangalore in the afternoon.
    • Reach Bangalore by late night, concluding your solo trip to Dandeli.

Google Ad 3

Please note:

  • Check the weather conditions before your trip and plan outdoor activities accordingly.
  • Respect the natural habitat and follow guidelines during wildlife safaris and treks.
  • Ensure you have necessary permits and follow safety guidelines for adventure activities.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo trip to Dandeli.
