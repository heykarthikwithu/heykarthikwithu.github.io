Bangalore to Sakleshpur Solo Trip: Complete Travel Guide

ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Sakleshpur offers an ideal getaway amidst the serene Western Ghats, lush greenery and pleasant weather.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Bangalore to Sakleshpur

  • Departure from Bangalore
    • Start your journey early in the morning to avoid traffic and make the most of your day.
    • The distance between Bangalore and Sakleshpur is approximately 220 kilometers and the drive takes around 4-5 hours.
  • Arrival in Sakleshpur
    • Check into your accommodation in Sakleshpur.
    • Freshen up and take some rest after the journey.
  • Visit Manjarabad Fort
    • Head to Manjarabad Fort, a historical site known for its unique star-shaped architecture and panoramic views.
    • Explore the fort, learn about its history and enjoy the scenic surroundings.
  • Sunset Point
    • Proceed to a nearby sunset point, such as Agni Gudda or Bettada Byraveshwara Temple.
    • Witness the mesmerizing sunset over the Western Ghats and capture some memorable photographs.

Explore Sakleshpur

  • Trek to Bisle Ghat Viewpoint
    • Start your day with a trek to Bisle Ghat Viewpoint, known for its stunning vistas of the Western Ghats and the Bisle Reserve Forest.
    • Enjoy the scenic beauty and serenity of the surroundings.
  • Visit Jenukal Gudda
    • Explore Jenukal Gudda, the second-highest peak in Karnataka, offering breathtaking views of the valleys and hills.
    • Engage in photography or simply soak in the tranquil ambiance.
  • Return to Sakleshpur
    • Return to Sakleshpur town and spend the evening exploring the local markets or relaxing at your accommodation.

Departure

  • Coffee Estate Tour
    • Start your day with a visit to a nearby coffee estate.
    • Learn about coffee cultivation, processing and tasting sessions.
  • Manjehalli Waterfalls
    • Visit Manjehalli Waterfalls, a hidden gem surrounded by lush greenery.
    • Enjoy a refreshing dip in the natural pool and soak in the beauty of the waterfall.
  • Return Journey to Bangalore
    • Check out from your accommodation.
    • Start your journey back to Bangalore in the afternoon.
    • Reach Bangalore by evening, concluding your solo trip to Sakleshpur.

Please note:

  • Check the weather conditions before your trip and plan outdoor activities accordingly.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions during your stay in Sakleshpur.
  • Ensure you have necessary permits and follow safety guidelines for trekking and waterfall visits.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo trip to Sakleshpur.
