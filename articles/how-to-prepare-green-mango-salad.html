How to prepare Green Mango Salad

ByReddamma D | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Green Mango Salad, also known as Som Tum in Thai cuisine, is a refreshing and tangy dish that combines the tartness of green mangoes with the crunch of vegetables and the kick of spices. It's perfect as a light meal or a side dish.

Here’s how to prepare a delicious Green Mango Salad at home:

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium green mangoes, peeled and julienned
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled and julienned (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts, coarsely crushed
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2-3 Thai bird chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2-3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (or soy sauce for a vegetarian/vegan option)
  • 1-2 tablespoons palm sugar or brown sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon dried shrimp (optional)
  • 1/4 cup green beans, cut into 1-inch pieces (optional)

Instructions:

  • Prepare the Green Mango:
    • Peel the green mangoes and cut them into thin julienne strips. You can use a julienne peeler or a sharp knife for this. Place the julienned mangoes in a large mixing bowl.
    • If using carrots, julienne them as well and add them to the bowl with the mangoes.
  • Prepare the Dressing:
    • In a small bowl, combine lime juice, fish sauce (or soy sauce) and palm sugar. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
    • Adjust the dressing to taste, balancing the sour, salty and sweet flavors to your preference.
  • Combine Ingredients:
    • Add the minced garlic and chopped Thai bird chilies to the bowl with the mangoes.
    • Add the halved cherry tomatoes, crushed roasted peanuts and chopped coriander leaves.
    • If using dried shrimp, add it now. If using green beans, add them as well.
  • Toss the Salad:
    • Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients in the bowl.
    • Toss everything together gently but thoroughly, ensuring the dressing coats all the ingredients evenly.
  • Serve:
    • Transfer the salad to a serving platter or individual plates.
    • Garnish with additional crushed peanuts and fresh coriander leaves if desired.
    • Serve immediately as a refreshing appetizer or side dish.

Additional Tips:

  • Adjusting Spice Level: You can adjust the number of Thai bird chilies according to your spice tolerance. For a milder version, use fewer chilies or remove the seeds.
  • Variations: You can add other vegetables like julienned cucumbers or bell peppers for additional color and crunch.
  • Protein Addition: For a more substantial salad, consider adding cooked and shredded chicken, shrimp, or tofu.
  • Serving Suggestions: Green Mango Salad pairs well with grilled meats, seafood, or as part of a Thai-inspired meal.

Enjoy this tangy, spicy and refreshing Green Mango Salad that brings together the best of fresh flavors and textures.
