Bangalore to Kovalam Solo Trip: Complete Travel Itinerary

ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Kovalam offers a perfect blend of relaxation, beach vibes and cultural experiences.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Route

Bangalore -> Salem -> Madurai -> Tirunelveli -> Nagercoil -> Kovalam

Bangalore to Kovalam

  • Departure from Bangalore
    • Start your journey early in the morning to make the most of your day.
    • The distance between Bangalore and Kovalam is approximately 700 kilometers and the drive takes around 12-14 hours.
  • Arrival in Kovalam
    • Check into your accommodation in Kovalam.
    • Freshen up and relax for a bit.
  • Kovalam Beach
    • Head to the iconic Kovalam Beach to witness a mesmerizing sunset.
    • Take a leisurely stroll along the shoreline and soak in the beach vibes.

Explore Kovalam

  • Yoga and Ayurveda
    • Start your day with a rejuvenating yoga session on the beach.
    • Explore Ayurvedic treatments and therapies available in Kovalam for relaxation and wellness.
  • Lighthouse Beach
    • Visit the Lighthouse Beach, one of the most popular beaches in Kovalam.
    • Enjoy water sports activities like swimming, surfing, or simply relax under the sun.
  • Local Cuisine
    • Indulge in delicious seafood at one of the beachside restaurants.
    • Explore the local markets and pick up souvenirs.

Day Trip to Trivandrum

  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple
    • Visit the iconic Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Trivandrum, known for its architectural beauty and religious significance.
  • Napier Museum and Zoo
    • Explore the Napier Museum to admire its collection of historical artifacts and art pieces.
    • Visit the nearby Trivandrum Zoo to see a variety of animal species.
  • Return to Kovalam
    • Head back to Kovalam and spend the evening relaxing at the beach or indulging in Ayurvedic treatments.

Departure to Bangalore

  • Spend the morning at leisure, perhaps revisiting a favorite spot or taking a final stroll along the beach.
  • Return Journey to Bangalore, Check out from your accommodation, start your journey back to Bangalore in the afternoon, reach Bangalore by late night.

Please note:

  • Check the weather conditions before your trip and plan outdoor activities accordingly.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions during your stay in Kovalam.
  • Ensure you have necessary permits and follow safety guidelines for water sports activities.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo trip to Kovalam.
