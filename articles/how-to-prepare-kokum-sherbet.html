How to prepare Kokum Sherbet

ByReddamma D | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Kokum Sherbet is a tangy and refreshing drink made from kokum (Garcinia indica), a fruit commonly used in Indian cuisine, especially in coastal regions. It's not only delicious but also has cooling properties, making it a perfect drink for hot weather.

Here’s a simple recipe to prepare Kokum Sherbet:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup dried kokum (about 8-10 pieces)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 to 3/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon black salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

1. Prepare Kokum Extract:

  • Rinse the dried kokum pieces under running water to remove any impurities.
  • In a bowl, soak the rinsed kokum in 4 cups of warm water for about 30-45 minutes.
  • This will help to extract the color and flavor from the kokum.

2. Strain the Mixture:

  • After soaking, use a strainer or muslin cloth to strain the kokum water into a pitcher, pressing the kokum pieces to extract as much liquid as possible.
  • Discard the solid kokum pieces.

3. Sweeten the Sherbet:

  • Add sugar to the kokum extract in the pitcher.
  • Stir well until the sugar dissolves completely.
  • You can adjust the sweetness according to your taste.

4. Season the Drink:

  • Add black salt and roasted cumin powder to the mixture.
  • Stir well to combine all the flavors.

5. Chill and Serve:

  • You can refrigerate the Kokum Sherbet for a couple of hours or serve it immediately over ice cubes.
  • Stir well before serving, as some ingredients may settle at the bottom.

6. Garnish: Pour the Kokum Sherbet into glasses and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.

7. Enjoy: Serve the Kokum Sherbet chilled as a refreshing drink.

Tips:

  • Variations: You can also add a pinch of chat masala for extra flavor.
  • Kokum Concentrate: If you have kokum concentrate, you can use it as a substitute for the dried kokum, following the package instructions for dilution.
  • Health Benefits: Kokum is known for its cooling properties, aids digestion, and is rich in antioxidants.

Kokum Sherbet is a delightful and revitalizing drink, perfect for hot summer days or anytime you need a refreshing beverage.
