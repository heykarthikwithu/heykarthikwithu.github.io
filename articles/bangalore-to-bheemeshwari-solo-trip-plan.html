Bangalore to Bheemeshwari: Solo Nature Trip & Fishing Guide

ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Bheemeshwari can be a peaceful and adventurous experience, as you'll get to explore the serene nature, engage in activities like fishing and river rafting and enjoy the tranquility of this riverside destination in Karnataka. Here's a suggested itinerary for your trip:

Route

Bangalore -> Ramanagara -> Channapatna -> Halagur -> Channapatna -> Ramanagara -> Bangalore

Bangalore -> Kanakapura -> Halagur -> Bangalore

Day 1: Bangalore to Bheemeshwari

  • Start your trip early in the morning from Bangalore.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Bheemeshwari is approximately 100 kilometers and it takes around 2-3 hours by road.
  • Take the route via Kanakapura to reach Bheemeshwari.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive through the countryside and the forested landscapes of Karnataka.
  • Reach Bheemeshwari by late morning.
  • Check into your accommodation and take some time to relax and freshen up.
  • In the afternoon, you can indulge in various outdoor activities such as fishing, nature walks, or river rafting in the Cauvery River.
  • Explore the peaceful surroundings and enjoy the tranquility of Bheemeshwari.
  • Spend the night in Bheemeshwari.

Day 2: Adventure and Nature

  • Start your day by engaging in adventure activities in Bheemeshwari.
  • You can go for a guided jungle trek, where you can spot a variety of flora and fauna.
  • Enjoy birdwatching, as Bheemeshwari is home to numerous bird species.
  • Engage in activities like kayaking or coracle rides in the Cauvery River.
  • If you're interested, you can also try your hand at zip-lining or rope walking.
  • In the afternoon, you can have a picnic lunch by the riverside and enjoy the serene beauty of the surroundings.
  • Spend the evening relaxing amidst nature and soaking in the peaceful ambiance of Bheemeshwari.
  • Spend another night in Bheemeshwari.

Day 3: Departure

  • Start your day by visiting the nearby Doddamakali Nature Camp, known for its scenic beauty and wildlife sightings.
  • Take a leisurely walk along the riverbank and enjoy the natural beauty.
  • If time permits, you can visit the nearby Kokrebellur Bird Sanctuary, which is known for its large population of painted storks and spot-billed pelicans.
  • In the afternoon, start your journey back to Bangalore.
  • Reach Bangalore by late evening and conclude your solo trip to Bheemeshwari with beautiful memories.

Please note that this itinerary provides a general outline for your trip and you can modify it according to your preferences and the time you have available. Make sure to check the current travel guidelines, road conditions and weather forecasts before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo adventure in Bheemeshwari.
