How to prepare Chana Chaat

ByReddamma D | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Chana Chaat is a delicious, tangy and healthy Indian street food made with chickpeas, fresh vegetables and flavorful spices. It's quick to prepare and perfect as a snack or light meal.

Here's a detailed recipe to prepare Chana Chaat at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (or 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 medium cucumber, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 medium boiled potato, peeled and diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sev (crunchy chickpea noodles) for garnish
  • 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind chutney (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chickpeas:
    • If using dried chickpeas, rinse them thoroughly and soak them in water overnight or for at least 8 hours.
    • Drain the soaked chickpeas and transfer them to a pressure cooker. Add enough water to cover the chickpeas and a pinch of salt. Cook for about 15-20 minutes or until the chickpeas are tender. If using a stovetop, it may take longer to cook the chickpeas until they are soft.
    • If using canned chickpeas, simply drain and rinse them under running water.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Finely chop the onion, tomato, cucumber and green chili (if using).
    • Peel and dice the boiled potato.
    • Chop the fresh coriander leaves.
    • If using, remove the seeds from the pomegranate.
  3. Assemble the Chaat:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked chickpeas, chopped onion, tomato, cucumber, green chili, boiled potato and pomegranate seeds.
    • Add the lemon juice, tamarind chutney (if using), chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, red chili powder (if using) and salt.
    • Mix everything well to combine all the flavors.
  4. Serve:
    • Transfer the Chana Chaat to serving plates or bowls.
    • Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves and sev for an added crunch.
    • Serve immediately to enjoy the fresh and tangy flavors.

Additional Tips:

  • Variations: You can add other vegetables like grated carrots, finely chopped bell peppers, or even boiled corn for added nutrition and variety.
  • Spice Level: Adjust the green chili and red chili powder according to your spice preference.
  • Chutneys: You can also add a drizzle of green chutney (coriander-mint chutney) for extra flavor.
  • Yogurt: For a creamy version, you can add a few tablespoons of whisked yogurt to the chaat.

Chana Chaat is a versatile dish that can be customized with different ingredients and flavors. It's not only delicious but also packed with nutrients, making it a perfect healthy snack or appetizer. Enjoy your homemade Chana Chaat.
