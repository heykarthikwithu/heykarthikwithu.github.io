Healthy diet to keep your teeth healthy

ByJyothi Karthik D | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

For a healthy tooth a person will need vitamins, calcium, fluoride and phosphorus to be taken regularly in their diet.

Here are a few healthy foods and their contents that keep your teeth stronger.

  • Vitamin A
    • Kale, Papaya, Carrot, Pumpkin.
  • Vitamin C
    • Oranges, Lemon, Cabbage, Cauliflower.
  • Vitamin D
    • Egg and Salmon Fish.
  • Calcium
    • Milk, Cheese, Yogurt.
  • Fluoride
    • Squid and Marina Fish.
  • Phosphorus
    • Liver and Mussel.

By having these vitamins, calcium, fluoride and phosphorus in your regular diet, you can make sure your oral health is at its best and healthy.
