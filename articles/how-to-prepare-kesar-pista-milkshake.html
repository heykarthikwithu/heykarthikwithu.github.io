How to prepare Kesar Pista Milkshake

ByReddamma D | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Kesar Pista Milkshake is a rich and creamy Indian beverage made with saffron (kesar), pistachios (pista), and chilled milk. This royal drink is not only a treat for the taste buds but also packed with nutritional benefits. It’s perfect for summers, festive occasions, or just when you're in the mood for something indulgent and refreshing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chilled full cream milk
  • 2 tablespoons pistachios (unsalted, shelled)
  • 10–12 saffron strands (kesar)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Crushed pistachios and saffron strands for garnish

Preparation Method

1. Soak Saffron and Pistachios

  • Soak saffron strands in 2 tablespoons of warm milk for 10–15 minutes to release their color and aroma.
  • Meanwhile, soak pistachios in warm water for 10 minutes. Peel off the skins for a smoother texture (optional).

2. Blend the Ingredients

  • In a blender, add:
    • Peeled pistachios
    • Soaked saffron milk
    • Sugar
    • Cardamom powder (if using)
    • Chilled milk
  • Blend until smooth and creamy. Add a few ice cubes if you like it extra cold.

3. Taste and Adjust

  • Check for sweetness and thickness. You can add more sugar or milk to adjust the consistency and flavor.

4. Serve and Garnish

  • Pour the milkshake into glasses.
  • Garnish with crushed pistachios and a few saffron strands on top.
  • Serve immediately for best taste and freshness.

Tips and Variations

  • Vegan Option: Replace dairy milk with almond milk or oat milk.
  • Thicker Milkshake: Add a scoop of vanilla or kulfi ice cream while blending.
  • Nut Mix: You can add a few soaked almonds or cashews for a richer taste.
  • Flavored Touch: Add a drop of rose water or kewra essence for a floral note.

Health Benefits of Kesar Pista Milkshake

  • Saffron: Contains antioxidants and is known to boost mood and support heart health.
  • Pistachios: A great source of healthy fats, protein, fiber, and antioxidants.
  • Milk: Provides calcium, vitamin D, and protein – essential for bone and muscle health.

Conclusion

Kesar Pista Milkshake is more than just a drink — it's an experience of luxury in a glass. With the richness of nuts, the exotic aroma of saffron, and the goodness of milk, it’s a royal treat that’s both satisfying and nourishing. Perfect for summer days, festive celebrations, or simply when you want to pamper yourself — sip, relax, and enjoy!
