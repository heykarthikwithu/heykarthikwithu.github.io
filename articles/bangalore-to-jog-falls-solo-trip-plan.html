Bangalore to Jog Falls: Solo Travel Itinerary & Guide

ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Jog Falls can be a refreshing experience, especially for nature lovers. Jog Falls, located in the Shimoga district of Karnataka, is one of the highest plunge waterfalls in India.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Bangalore to Jog Falls

  • Begin your journey early in the morning to make the most of the day.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Jog Falls is approximately 400 kilometers and the drive takes around 8-9 hours.
  • Reach Jog Falls by late morning or early afternoon.
  • Check into your accommodation and freshen up.
  • Head to the viewpoints to witness the grandeur of Jog Falls.
  • Visit the Watkins Platform, from where you can get a panoramic view of the falls.
  • Explore the area around the falls and enjoy the natural beauty.
  • Spend the evening near the falls, enjoying the sunset.
  • Relax and unwind at your accommodation.

Nature and Local Exploration

  • If you enjoy early mornings, consider heading back to the falls to witness the sunrise.
  • Explore nearby attractions like the Linganamakki Dam.
  • If you're interested in wildlife, visit the Tyavarekoppa Lion and Tiger Safari.
  • Spend the afternoon at leisure.
  • Try local cuisine at a nearby eatery.
  • Start your journey back to Bangalore in the evening.
  • Reach Bangalore by late night.

Please note:

  • Check the timings and accessibility of Jog Falls before planning your trip.
  • Verify road conditions and take necessary precautions, especially if you're driving.
  • Consider the weather, as monsoon season can be the best time to witness Jog Falls in its full glory.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo trip to Jog Falls.
