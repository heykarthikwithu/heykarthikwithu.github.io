Courtship time, Make it memorable, ways to add some spice

ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Courtship is one of the most beautiful phases of a relationship. It is the time between saying “yes” and finally getting married. For many couples, this period brings excitement, curiosity, new emotions, and a lot of dreams about the future. Even if the wedding date is months away, this time can be used to build a strong connection and create sweet memories that last for life.

Here are a few simple and meaningful ways to make your courtship period special.

1. Wedding Planning – The Perfect Time Pass

Wedding planning may feel stressful sometimes, but it is actually one of the best bonding activities for couples.
Every task—whether choosing outfits, booking venues, or finalising décor—gives time to talk, meet, and understand each other better.

Instead of just “getting the work done,” couples can plan a small moment around every task.
For example:

  • If you both are going to buy a wedding dress, plan to get matching colours for fun.
  • After shopping, go on a short walk or have a coffee date.
  • Click some cute photos together to remember these days later.

Wedding planning becomes more fun when both treat it as a shared journey rather than a responsibility.

2. Talk About Family and Friends

Knowing each other’s family and friends helps build comfort even before the marriage.
Have simple conversations like:

  • How the day goes at home
  • Who handles what work
  • Funny stories from childhood
  • Friends they are close to
  • The kind of bond they have with siblings or parents

These talks make both feel included in each other’s world.
When the wedding comes closer, these connections will feel natural and warm.

3. Leave Small Hints About What You Like

No one is a mind-reader. Couples should not expect the other person to magically know personal likes and expectations.
Leaving small hints makes things easy and avoids confusion in the future.

Share small things like:

  • What makes you happy
  • What annoys you
  • What kind of gifts you enjoy
  • How you prefer spending weekends
  • Your favourite foods, colours, movies, or places

These small details help both understand each other on a deeper level and make future surprises meaningful.

4. Stop Being Shy – Flirt a Little

Courtship is the time when the relationship gets playful and romantic.
A little flirting makes the bond lively and exciting.

Simple compliments always work:

  • “You look great today.”
  • “I like the way you talk.”
  • “You make my day better.”

These small words build confidence and bring closeness.
Honest compliments show that both appreciate each other not just for looks, but for thoughts, behaviour, and personality.

5. Plan Small Surprises

Surprises do not always have to be grand or expensive.
Even a simple handwritten note or a small gift can bring a big smile.

A few easy surprise ideas:

  • Send their favourite snack
  • Record a voice message when they least expect it
  • Plan a small date after a long day
  • Gift a book, perfume, or something meaningful
  • Create a playlist of songs that remind you of them

Surprises add freshness to the relationship.
And yes… it’s true that men may not always be the best at planning surprises, so even a tiny effort feels special.

6. Share Future Dreams and Plans

Courtship is the best time to discuss the future—both big and small things.
Talk about dreams, career goals, places to travel, and how life should look after marriage.

These discussions help avoid misunderstandings later and make both feel aligned.

7. Respect Each Other’s Space and Pace

Even in a close relationship, people need personal space.
Some prefer fast bonding, while some take time to open up.
Respecting each other’s pace builds trust and comfort.

Giving space shows maturity and makes the relationship healthier.

8. Create Little Rituals Together

Small habits become big memories later.
Some simple rituals couples enjoy during courtship:

  • A nightly 10-minute call
  • Sending a good morning message
  • Sharing one highlight of the day
  • Watching the same show and discussing it
  • Weekly meet-ups for short walks or dinner

These rituals build a routine of togetherness.

Conclusion

Courtship time is a once-in-a-lifetime phase. It is the period where emotions are fresh, hopes are high, and the relationship feels new and exciting. Making it memorable does not require big efforts—just honest communication, small surprises, shared activities, and a little romance.
