Bangalore to Yercaud Scenic Drive – The Perfect Weekend Road Trip from Bengaluru

By Karthik Kumar D K | Published: Jul 21, 2026 11:11 AM IST
By Karthik Kumar D K , 21 July 2026
Route Map from Bangalore to Yercaud

Looking for a refreshing weekend getaway from Bangalore that combines smooth highways, winding mountain roads, breathtaking viewpoints, pleasant weather, and peaceful nature? A Bangalore to Yercaud scenic drive is one of South India's most rewarding road trips. Located in the Shevaroy Hills of Tamil Nadu, Yercaud is often called the "Poor Man's Ooty", not because it lacks beauty, but because it offers a similar hill station experience without overwhelming crowds or expensive accommodation.

Whether you're riding solo on a motorcycle, driving as a couple, or traveling with family, Yercaud offers an ideal escape from Bengaluru's busy city life. The journey itself is part of the adventure, with excellent highways leading to Salem, followed by one of the most enjoyable hill climbs in South India featuring 20 beautifully engineered hairpin bends.

This guide covers everything you need to know, including the best route, driving tips, attractions, viewpoints, places to eat, accommodation suggestions, travel budget, safety advice, and an ideal two-day itinerary.

Why Visit Yercaud?

Unlike many crowded hill stations, Yercaud still retains a calm and relaxed atmosphere.

Visitors come here to enjoy:

  • Pleasant weather throughout the year
  • Scenic mountain roads
  • Coffee plantations
  • Orange groves
  • Beautiful viewpoints
  • Peaceful lakes
  • Short trekking trails
  • Family-friendly attractions
  • Budget-friendly accommodation
  • Less traffic compared to Ooty or Kodaikanal

For people living in Bengaluru, it makes an excellent one or two-day road trip.

Distance from Bangalore to Yercaud

  • Distance: Approximately 230–250 km (depending on your starting point)
  • Travel Time: Around 5 to 6 hours
  • Best Start Time: Between 5:00 AM and 6:00 AM

Leaving early helps you avoid Bengaluru traffic and reach the hill station before afternoon.

Route

Bangalore → Electronic City → Hosur → Dharmapuri → Salem → Yercaud

OR via Krishnagiri
(Bangalore → Electronic City → Hosur → Krishnagiri → Dharmapuri → Salem → Yercaud)

This is the fastest and most comfortable route via NH 44, one of India's best national highways.

The roads remain in excellent condition throughout most of the journey.

Bangalore to Salem

The first part of the journey is smooth highway driving.

You'll pass:

  • Electronic City
  • Hosur
  • Krishnagiri
  • Dharmapuri

There are numerous fuel stations, restaurants, and rest stops along the highway.

Popular breakfast stops include:

  • A2B
  • Saravana Bhavan
  • Sri Krishna Inn
  • Hotel Anand Bhavan

Krishnagiri is an ideal location for breakfast before continuing towards Salem.

Salem to Yercaud – The Real Adventure Begins

Once you reach Salem, the drive changes dramatically.

The climb to Yercaud begins immediately after leaving the city.

The road features 20 famous hairpin bends, each numbered, making the drive both exciting and memorable.

Unlike many hill roads, these bends are wide and well maintained.

During the climb you'll enjoy:

  • Dense forests
  • Coffee estates
  • Valley views
  • Cool mountain breeze
  • Monkeys along the roadside
  • Excellent photography opportunities

Take your time.

The climb usually takes around 40–50 minutes.

Driving Tips for the Hairpin Bends

Although the road is safe, careful driving is important.

  • Use lower gears while climbing.
  • Avoid unnecessary overtaking.
  • Stay within your lane.
  • Watch for buses around blind curves.
  • Avoid stopping in the middle of bends.
  • Check brakes before descending.
  • Riders should avoid leaning aggressively.

If you're traveling by motorcycle, the climb is one of the highlights of the trip.

Travel Tips

  • Leave early to avoid Bengaluru traffic.
  • Carry cash as some small shops may not accept digital payments.
  • Wear light jackets during winter.
  • Keep your fuel tank adequately filled before climbing.
  • Download offline maps, as mobile signals may fluctuate in some areas.
  • Start descending before dark if you're not staying overnight.
  • Respect wildlife and avoid feeding monkeys.

Safety Tips

  • Drive carefully on hairpin bends.
  • Avoid speeding.
  • Stay hydrated during summer.
  • Use engine braking while descending.
  • Keep emergency contacts handy.
  • Check weather forecasts before traveling during the monsoon.
  • Wear proper riding gear if traveling by motorcycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Yercaud suitable for beginners driving in hills?

Yes. The roads are among the safest hill roads in South India, provided you drive carefully.

2. How many hairpin bends are there?

There are 20 numbered hairpin bends on the climb from Salem to Yercaud.

3. Can I visit Yercaud in one day?

Yes, but an overnight stay allows you to enjoy sunrise, sunset, and the peaceful atmosphere without rushing.

4. Is parking available at tourist attractions?

Yes. Most major attractions, including Yercaud Lake, Lady's Seat, Pagoda Point, Shevaroy Temple, and Anna Park, have designated parking areas. Weekend crowds may make parking slightly more challenging, so arriving early is helpful.

5. Is Yercaud good for solo travelers?

Absolutely. It is considered one of the safer hill stations in South India and is popular among solo riders, photographers, and nature lovers.

Final Thoughts

A Bangalore to Yercaud scenic drive is much more than just a road trip—it's an opportunity to leave behind the noise of city life and reconnect with nature. From the smooth highways of NH 44 to the thrilling ascent through 20 picturesque hairpin bends, every stage of the journey offers something memorable. Once you arrive, Yercaud welcomes you with cool mountain air, coffee plantations, panoramic viewpoints, peaceful lakes, and a slower pace of life that encourages relaxation.

Whether you're planning a solo motorcycle adventure, a romantic weekend as a couple, or a family getaway, Yercaud delivers a rewarding experience without requiring extensive planning or a large budget. Its accessibility from Bengaluru, combined with its natural beauty and year-round pleasant climate, makes it one of the finest weekend destinations in South India.

If you're looking for a scenic drive that blends adventure, tranquility, and unforgettable hill station charm, Yercaud deserves a place at the top of your travel list. Pack your bags, fuel up your vehicle, and get ready to enjoy one of the most scenic road trips from Bangalore.
