Bangalore to Nagarahole Solo Trip Plan

ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Nagarahole promises an exciting adventure amidst the wildlife and natural beauty of the national park. Nagarahole, also known as Rajiv Gandhi National Park, is renowned for its diverse flora and fauna.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Bangalore to Nagarahole

  • Begin your journey early in the morning to allow ample time for exploration.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Nagarahole is approximately 220 kilometers and the drive takes around 5-6 hours.
  • Reach Nagarahole by late morning or early afternoon.
  • Check into your accommodation. There are several wildlife resorts around the park that offer a comfortable stay.
  • Start your adventure with a jungle safari in Nagarahole National Park.
  • Explore the wildlife sanctuary and keep an eye out for elephants, tigers, leopards and various bird species.
  • Enjoy a nature walk in the serene surroundings.
  • Some areas within the park might offer boat safaris, providing a different perspective of the wildlife.

Wildlife Exploration

  • Begin your day with an early morning safari for a higher chance of spotting wildlife.
  • Experience the tranquility of the jungle during the early hours.
  • Spend some time relaxing at your accommodation.
  • Visit the Nagarahole Interpretation Center to learn more about the park's flora and fauna.
  • Spend the evening by the riverside, enjoying the peaceful ambiance.
  • Reflect on the day's wildlife sightings and natural beauty.

Departure

  • Explore one of the nature trails within the park.
  • Engage in bird watching, as Nagarahole is home to a variety of bird species.
  • Check out from your accommodation.
  • Start your journey back to Bangalore in the late morning or early afternoon.
  • Reach Bangalore by the evening, concluding your solo trip.

Please note:

  • Check the safari timings and availability in advance.
  • Follow responsible wildlife viewing practices.
  • Respect the rules and guidelines of the national park for a safe and enjoyable experience.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo adventure in Nagarahole.
